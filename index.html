About NASO

The Nepal Astronomical Society (NASO) is a non-profit organization established in 2008 with the goal of promoting astronomy and space science in Nepal.

We organize various programs, workshops, and star parties to engage the public and students in astronomical activities. Our mission is to create a platform for astronomy enthusiasts and professionals to share knowledge and inspire the next generation of stargazers.

NASO is actively involved in national and international astronomy projects, working to put Nepal on the global astronomy map.
