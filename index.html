About YISS

Youth Innovative Science Society(YISS) is a non-profit organization established in 2019 with the goal of promoting science and technology in Itahari.

We organize various programs, outreach, and night sky observation to engage the public and students in science. Our mission is to create a platform to learn create and share thier knowledge to others.
