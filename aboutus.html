Welcome to Youth Innovative Science Society

Youth Innovative Science Society (YISS) is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting science and technology in the Khanar region and across Nepal. Founded in 2019, we have grown into a dynamic community of students, educators, and science enthusiasts working together to advance research, innovation, and public engagement.

Our mission is to inspire curiosity and nurture a scientific temper in people of all ages by opening doors to the fascinating world of science. We believe that exploring and understanding the universe not only deepens our appreciation of the cosmos but also encourages a lifelong spirit of inquiry, creativity, and discovery.

Through hands-on workshops, night-sky observations, interactive talks, and outreach programs in schools and communities, YISS seeks to make science accessible, exciting, and relevant. By connecting learners with real scientific experiences and fostering collaboration, we aim to empower the next generation of thinkers and innovators who will help shape a brighter, knowledge-driven future for Nepal and beyond.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a sustainable learning-teaching platform in Itahari that is open and accessible to every student group. We strive to spark curiosity, nurture critical thinking, and encourage hands-on exploration so that students of all backgrounds can discover their passions and grow into lifelong learners.

Our Vision

Our vision is to empower young minds to reach for higher aspirations and to share the beauty of the universe with people of all ages. By making night-sky observation and hands-on learning open to everyone, we aim to build a culture of curiosity and wonder.
